Research

I am broadly interested in quantum machine learning, statistical learning theory and privacy-preserving computation.
I work under the supervision of Prof. Elham Kashefi at the Edinburgh - Paris Virtual Quantum Lab. My doctoral contract is funded by QICS - Quantum Information Center Sorbonne.

About me

I was born and raised in Cava de' Tirreni, and I received a B.Sc. in Physics and a M.Sc. in Computer Science from Sapienza University of Rome. I have been an honor student at SSAS - Sapienza School for Advanced Studies.
During my master, I have also been a visiting student at École Normale Supérieure and a research assistant at Sorbonne University.
